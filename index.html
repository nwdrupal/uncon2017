What is an Unconference?

An unconference is the opposite of a conference. Where a conference has a schedule of talks planned in advance, an unconference starts with no agenda until the attendees, not the organisers, determine one.

Everyone is encouraged to bring a talk to the event, with the day having a 30 minute cadence including time for questions, and time for people to change rooms ready for the next talk to begin. Our venue is a flexible working space, allowing us to open many tracks simultaneously as required, and facilitate as many talks as possible.

The focus of the unconference is the Drupal Content Management Framework, and supporting subjects including databases, frameworks, security, UX, front-end development and design are more than welcome.
